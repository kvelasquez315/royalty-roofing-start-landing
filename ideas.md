**Approach 1: Commanding Authority**

- **Design Movement:** American Craftsman meets Corporate Authority — think bold editorial layouts used by premium construction firms.
- **Core Principles:** Trust through visual weight; dark navy dominance; gold accents as a royalty signal; zero clutter.
- **Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0F1B2D) as the commanding base. Warm gold (#C9A84C) for royalty/crown motifs. Off-white (#F5F2EC) for breathing room. The emotional intent is "this company has been here for 15 years and will outlast everyone else."
- **Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric split-screen hero — photo bleeds left edge, form floats right with a dark card. Sections alternate between full-bleed dark and off-white panels to create rhythm.
- **Signature Elements:** Crown motif echoing the logo; bold condensed numerals for stats (4.9 / 500+); horizontal rule dividers in gold.
- **Interaction Philosophy:** Minimal animation — only the CTA button pulses softly on load. Everything else is static and confident.
- **Animation:** Fade-up entrance for hero text (200ms, ease-out). CTA button has a subtle gold shimmer on hover. No scroll animations.
- **Typography System:** "Barlow Condensed" (700) for headlines — strong, compressed, authoritative. "DM Sans" (400/500) for body — clean, readable, modern.

**Approach 2: Neighborhood Trust Builder**

- **Design Movement:** Midwest Honest — the visual language of a company that shows up on time, doesn't upsell, and has been in your neighborhood for 15 years.
- **Core Principles:** Warmth over flash; real photography front-and-center; social proof as the primary visual element; frictionless form.
- **Color Philosophy:** Slate blue (#1E3A5F) as primary — trustworthy, not corporate. Warm white (#FAFAF8) backgrounds. Amber (#F59E0B) for stars and accents only. Feels like a firm handshake.
- **Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero photo with a semi-transparent overlay strip at the bottom holding the headline. Below: a 3-column trust strip. Then alternating left/right content blocks with photos. No sidebar layouts.
- **Signature Elements:** Real team photos in circular frames with name + title; star ratings rendered large; "Best of Omaha" badges in a horizontal scrolling strip.
- **Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth scroll anchoring. Phone number is always visible in a sticky top bar. Form fields highlight in amber on focus.
- **Animation:** Hero overlay fades in over 400ms. Trust strip counters count up on scroll-into-view. Card hover lifts with shadow.
- **Typography System:** "Playfair Display" (700) for section headlines — dignified, local. "Source Sans 3" (400/600) for body — highly legible at small sizes.

**Approach 3: Premium Conversion Machine (CHOSEN)**

- **Design Movement:** High-Performance Landing Page — inspired by the best-converting home service pages (GAF, Owens Corning certified contractors). Every pixel earns its place by driving a call or form fill.
- **Core Principles:** Message match with Google Ads; above-the-fold form; social proof stacked in layers; urgency without gimmicks.
- **Color Philosophy:** Navy (#0F1B2D) primary — serious, established. Royal blue (#3D6CC0) for interactive elements — active, trustworthy. Off-white (#F7F6F3) for content sections. The palette mirrors royaltyroofing.org exactly for ad-to-page message match.
- **Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport hero with house photo background, dark overlay, headline left, form card right (sticky on desktop). Below the fold: horizontal trust bar → "Why Royalty" section → Reviews grid → Team strip → Bottom form → Footer. No sidebar — pure vertical scroll with clear visual hierarchy.
- **Signature Elements:** Sticky phone number in navbar; "Best of Omaha" badge strip with all 7 years; form with inline validation and success state.
- **Interaction Philosophy:** Every CTA scrolls to the bottom form. Phone number is a tap-to-call link everywhere. Form submit shows a thank-you message in-place (no page reload).
- **Animation:** Hero text slides up on load (300ms). Stats in trust bar count up when entering viewport. Form fields have smooth focus transitions. No distracting scroll animations.
- **Typography System:** "Barlow" (800/700) for hero headline — bold, direct, high-impact. "DM Sans" (400/500/600) for all body, form labels, and nav — matches the brand site exactly.

## Selected Approach: **Approach 3 — Premium Conversion Machine**

This approach is chosen because it directly serves the Google Ads conversion goal. Every design decision is justified by conversion rate optimization principles: message match, above-the-fold form, layered social proof, and frictionless CTAs.
